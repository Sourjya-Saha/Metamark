Aishwarya, a beautiful, aspiring girl, who had just completed her bachelors in archeology, starts her first job at an excavation site. She finds a mysterious pot that seems worthless to her colleagues. Without considering those comments, she starts doing research about it and finds an unimaginable mystery behind it. Before she could actually tell the world what that is, she dies in an accident. All she leaves behind are a few comments about her research to a playful girl, her sister, Athvika. A few months pass and Athvika marries Eashwar, a computer genius and the owner of a billion-dollar company. Eashwar always digs a problem to the core if he finds something wrong with it. Marriage life does not go as good as expected. Athvika always seems distraught and never opens up to Eashwar. He volunteers himself to help Athvika recover from her stress. The story starts to take a turn once the mastermind gets involved in the game.

About the author

Follow authors to get new release updates, plus improved recommendations.

Reyhaan

Brief content visible, double tap to read full content.
Full content visible, double tap to read brief content.

Reyhaan is an accomplished Indian writer with over 12 years of experience in cinema screenplay writing, known for crafting emotionally engaging stories with depth, realism, and strong visual narration. His journey in storytelling began long before his professional career, driven by a lifelong fascination with human emotions, relationships, and the silent moments that define us.

Beyond writing, Reyhaan is also a music producer, blending rhythm and emotion to enhance the worlds he creates. His diverse creative background brings a cinematic, multi-layered texture to his books, making his stories vivid, modern, and deeply relatable.

A patent holder and an entrepreneur, Reyhaan combines creativity with innovation, consistently exploring new ideas and pushing boundaries in every field he steps into. His unique mix of artistic skill and problem-solving mindset shapes his writing with authenticity and originality.

Through his novels, Reyhaan aims to inspire, heal, and connect with readers across the worldone story at a time.
